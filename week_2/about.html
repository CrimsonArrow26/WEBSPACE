About Us

At Nivaran, we believe that solving everyday local problems shouldn't be a struggle. Our mission is to empower citizens to report issues around them—be it potholes, broken streetlights, garbage dumps, or water leakage—with just a few taps. Nivaran bridges the gap between people and problem-solvers by offering a simple, intuitive platform where users can mark issues directly on a map, describe them, and track updates as they get resolved. Whether you're a concerned resident or part of a responsible organization, Nivaran helps create more transparent, accountable, and livable communities. We’re not just an app we’re a movement to make neighborhoods better, one fix at a time.
